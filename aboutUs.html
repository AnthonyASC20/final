Our Mission

Tempus Salvis was created by a teenager to help students of all ages avoid the inevitable, procrastination. Tempus Salvis provides access to an unlimited array of educational videos with just a few clicks, making it easy for any student to get a head start on their approaching exam. Quickly switching to first person, I definitely understand the struggle of school, so I truly hope to make the grind a bit easier for any student who utilizes Tempus Salvis!
