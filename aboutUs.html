Our Mission

Tempus Salvis was created by 4 teenagers to help students of all ages avoid the inevitable, procrastination. Tempus Salvis provides access to an unlimited array of educational videos with just a few clicks, making it easy for any student to get a head start on their approaching exam. Quickly switching to first person, we here on the T.S. team already understand the struggle of school, so we really hope to make the grind a bit easier for any student who utilizes Tempus Salvis!
