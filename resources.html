Studying Tips

  • Space out your studying to avoid cramming all your review in the day before the big test.
  • Reread notes & chapters for understanding, rather than memorization.
  • After reviewing the material, apply what you've read to practice problems & quizzes.
  • Organize your study space to minimize distraction and maximize concentration.
  • Experiment studying with music; find which genres bolster your productivity.
  • ASK for help, regardless of how daunting the task may seem. I promise it will take a huge weight off your shoulders!
  • Try out visual studying practices, like color coding or mind maps.
